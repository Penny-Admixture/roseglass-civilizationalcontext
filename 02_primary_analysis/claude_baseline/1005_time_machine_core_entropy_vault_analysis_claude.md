### Technical Challenges by Category

**1. Physical Hardware Preservation**

**Sun SPARCstations**:
- **NVRAM battery replacement**: Must be done before boot (stores MAC address, hostid)
- **SCSI drive maintenance**: Drives may have seized bearings
- **Power supply capacitors**: Aging, may need recap
- **Monitor compatibility**: Sun used proprietary video signals (13W3 connectors)
- **Cooling**: Loud fans, possible failures
- **Space requirements**: Each unit ~20" deep, heavy

**Game Consoles**:
- **Pong console**: Discrete logic (no CPU), analog circuitry
- **Capacitor aging**: Electrolytic caps dry out after 50 years
- **RF modulator**: May not work with modern TVs
- **Controllers**: Potentiometer drift, mechanical wear

**Point-of-Sale**:
- **Proprietary hardware**: Non-standard connectors, locked firmware
