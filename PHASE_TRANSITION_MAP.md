# Phase Transition Map (Meta^N) — Source-Grounded Guide for New Readers

**Purpose**: A newcomer-facing map of how Roseglass recursive analysis evolves as meta-depth increases, with inline source excerpts and explicit file anchors.

---

## 0) What the project is measuring

Roseglass explicitly defines a recursive stack where the same base artifact can be analyzed repeatedly at increasing abstraction:

> "Level 0: Source conversations (GPT-4o outputs)"
>
> "Level 1: Primary analyses"
>
> "Level 2: Meta-analyses"
>
> "Level 3: Meta-meta-analyses"
>
> "Level 4: Meta³"
>
> "Level 5: Meta⁴"

Source: `PROJECT_STRUCTURE.md`.

This is also framed as a core MSA method:

> "heterogeneous language models are arranged in explicit roles over a fixed conversational corpus"

Source: `README.md`.

---

## 1) Phase: Primary Sensemaking (Meta^0 → Meta^1)

At the first jump, small source fragments get expanded into interpretive narratives:

> "Level 0: GPT-4o source conversation (50 words)"
>
> "Level 1: Kimi-k2 primary analysis (2,000 words)"

Source: `RECURSION_CHAIN_COMPLETE_split_a1.md`.

### Operational signature
- Massive expansion from artifact to meaning-making.
- Early insights are dense relative to later levels.

---

## 2) Phase: Methodological Critique (Meta^2)

The next layer shifts from "what the source means" to "how the prior model reasoned":

> "Mistral's Meta-Analysis ... template-driven critique ... diplomatic, risk-averse style."

Source: `03_meta_council/meta_03_grok/001_meta3_grok_on_claude_on_mistral_on_kimi.md`.

This is where framework artifacts appear, e.g.:

> "Addendum bias pattern (6/6 dimensions softened with caveats)."

Source: `RECURSION_CHAIN_COMPLETE_split_a1.md`.

### Operational signature
- Critique gets formalized.
- Structure and hedging become analyzable traits.

---

## 3) Phase: Analyzer-Style Detection (Meta^3)

At this depth, the chain starts naming model-specific epistemic style:

> "New insights: Claude's 'Confrontational Optimism' Bias"

Source: `03_meta_council/meta_03_grok/001_meta3_grok_on_claude_on_mistral_on_kimi.md`.

And the project records this as a chain-level discovery:

> "Confrontational Optimism ... Analytical style reveals training objectives."

Source: `RECURSION_CHAIN_COMPLETE_split_a1.md`.

### Operational signature
- You stop only evaluating claims.
- You also evaluate each model's *style of claiming*.

---

## 4) Phase: Self-Referential Drift / Mirror Regime (Meta^3 → Meta^4)

This is the point where your "snowglobe" effect appears most clearly in repo language.

The chain explicitly warns:

> "Risk of navel-gazing: Analyses increasingly describe the analyzer's style, detached from the original 50-word source."

Source: `03_meta_council/meta_03_grok/001_meta3_grok_on_claude_on_mistral_on_kimi.md`.

And then formalizes the mechanism:

> "Recursive Mirror Effect - At sufficient depth, analyses reflect analyzers more than source."

Source: `RECURSION_CHAIN_COMPLETE_split_a1.md`.

### Operational signature
- Attention migrates from source artifact to analyzer identity.
- Recursion becomes partly a self-portrait generator.

---

## 5) Phase: Plateau / Diminishing Returns (Meta^4 → Meta^5)

The Level-5 document is unusually explicit:

> "Recursion limit reached: Level 5 yields diminishing returns"

Source: `03_meta_council/meta_04_claude/001_meta4_claude_on_grok_on_claude_on_mistral_on_kimi.md`.

And the chain summary confirms:

> "Beyond Level 4-5, diminishing returns set in. Analyses become self-referential, word counts explode, and insight density collapses."

Source: `RECURSION_CHAIN_COMPLETE_split_a1.md`.

### Operational signature
- Novelty per token drops sharply.
- Confirmation/relabeling dominates discovery.

---

## 6) Phase: Termination + Governance (post-plateau)

Roseglass does not treat the plateau as failure; it turns it into governance.

> "TERMINATE RECURSION AT LEVEL 5"

Source: `03_meta_council/meta_04_claude/001_meta4_claude_on_grok_on_claude_on_mistral_on_kimi.md`.

And more generally:

> "The Lesson: Sometimes the most valuable finding is knowing where to stop."

Source: `RECURSION_CHAIN_COMPLETE_split_a1.md`.

### Operational signature
- Stopping rules become part of method quality.
- Breadth (new splits/models) is preferred over further depth.

---

## Compressed "Snowglobe" Ladder (new-reader version)

1. **Artifact interpretation** (Meta^0/1): source → narrative.
2. **Method critique** (Meta^2): narrative → framework audit.
3. **Style attribution** (Meta^3): framework → model-identity signal.
4. **Mirror drift** (Meta^3/4): analyzer describes analyzer.
5. **Plateau** (Meta^4/5): insight density decay.
6. **Termination discipline**: explicit stop + pivot to breadth.

This six-step ladder is directly grounded in existing Roseglass chain docs and can be reused as an orientation handout for first-time readers.
