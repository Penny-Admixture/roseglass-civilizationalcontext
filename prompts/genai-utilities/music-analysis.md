## SCHENKERIAN ANALYSIS NOTES

**What it is:**
- Analytical method by Heinrich Schenker
- Reveals underlying tonal structure
- Shows how surface complexity derives from simple foundations
- Hierarchical reduction to Ursatz (fundamental structure)

**Key concepts:**
- Foreground, middleground, background levels
- Prolongation of tonic harmony
- Linear progressions
- Structural vs. embellishing tones

**Application to popular music:**
- May need adaptation from classical origins
- Focus on tonal hierarchy
- Identify fundamental harmonic motion
- Map melodic elaboration
