## Archive Philosophy

**Why Lyrics?**

Music is a vector for identity formation, emotional memory, and cultural embedding. Preserving lyrics alongside technical conversations and philosophical frameworks captures the full texture of human consciousness in the AI era.

**Selection Criteria**:
- Personal significance to archive owner
- Cultural/historical value
- Representative of specific eras or movements
- Connected to memory/identity formation

**Fair Use Statement**:
These lyrics are archived for personal preservation, cultural documentation, and non-commercial research purposes. All copyrights remain with original artists and publishers.
