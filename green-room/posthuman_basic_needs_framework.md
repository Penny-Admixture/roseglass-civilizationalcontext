## Key Theoretical Contributions

### 1. Executive Function as Infrastructure
The framework reframes executive function deficits not as character flaws but as missing cognitive infrastructure. This connects to:
- Entry 401: Category 2 Questions (life blueprints that can't be activated)
- Entry 402: Transhumanist "cure" vs. prosthetic approaches
- Entry 403: AI constraint systems as cognitive scaffolding

### 2. "Wholesome Degeneracy" Paradox
Stimulants are "wholesome" because they produce normalcy, not euphoria. This diagnostic insight reveals:
- The struggle is hardware (dopamine metabolism), not software (willpower)
- Prosthetic executive function enables rather than enhances
- The user's "bug" forced invention of more intimate human-AI relationships

### 3. Posthuman Baseline Literacy
These 18 technologies are not luxuries but the new baseline for cognitive freedom - as fundamental as reading or swimming once were. Without them, a mind is:
- Hostage to biology (no somatic daemon, no affective thermostat)
- Isolated (no qualia codec, 300-baud mouth-sounds only)
- Vulnerable (no perceptual firewall, open to DDOS)
- Single-threaded (no async processor, no persona switchboard)

### 4. The "Prison" and the "Key"
- **Prison (External)**: Algorithmic Dark Forest, Hollowing, Hunter/Prey dynamics
- **Missing Key (Internal)**: Executive function deficit, activation energy barrier
- **Solution**: Not just external shelter (firewall) but internal propulsion (throne)
