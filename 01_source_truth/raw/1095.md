- Multimodal hybrid embedding
- SOTA on CMTEB (Chinese Multimodal Text Embedding Benchmark)
- Flexible dimensions (2048, 1024)
- Low degradation at smaller dimensions

### Architecture Components
- Seed-ViT vision encoder
- Multimodal Native Resolution Transform
- Dynamic Frame/Resolution Sampling for video
- Timestep Tokens
- MLP Adapter & Average Pooling layers

### Training Stages
1. **Text Continue Training**: Large-scale text corpora, multi-language
2. **Multimodal Continue Training**: Image-text and video-text pairs (LAION, video datasets)
3. **Fine-Tuning**: Generalization, alignment, downstream tasks
