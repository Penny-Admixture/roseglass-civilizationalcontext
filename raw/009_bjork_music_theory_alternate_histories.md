### 2. Modes vs Schenkerian Analysis
**User**: Asks about relationship between modes and Schenkerian analysis, calls it "basic bitch question"

**GPT-4o**:
- Modes = "flavors" of diatonic scale (Ionian, Dorian, Phrygian, Lydian, Mixolydian, Aeolian, Locrian)
- Schenkerian analysis = "deep structure" in tonal music, focused on Urlinie and bass arpeggiation (I-V-I)
- **Key insight**: Schenkerian analysis "strongly biased toward major/minor tonality"
- Modes don't behave hierarchically like Schenker expects
- Modes are "horizontal and color-driven" vs "function-driven"
- Analogy: Modes = emotional dialects, Schenker = syntax police saying "all poems secretly say the same thing"
